Generator Hub

Open Generators ▸ Generator Hub from the top menu, or choose one of the quick generator links to open the hub with that tool already selected. The hub is an editor-side brainstorming workspace: it does not change your project until you copy a result into a database entry, event, or your notes.

Included generators

The 20 original word banks cover Weapon, Armor, Spell, Currency, Item, Enemy, Character, Faction, Settlement, Kingdom, Dungeon, Tavern, Potion, Artifact, Quest, Deity, Ship, Book & Tome, Fantasy Plant, and Festival names. Every generator combines subtypes, tone words, concepts, materials, coined-name syllables, titles, and several templates, so even narrow filters retain thousands of possible results.

Shaping the results

  • Pick a generator-specific subtype such as blade, shield, curse, port, ruin, or sacred festival.
  • Choose Heroic, Dark, Mystical, Rustic, or Cosmic tones where they fit the subject.
  • Use Concise for short names, Evocative for layered names, or Legendary for formal titles.
  • Generate batches of 5, 10, or 20 results. Surprise me randomizes the generator and its style.
  • Turn on Alliteration, prefix names with The, or show one-line Story hooks.
  • Enter a World keyword to anchor results to a place, dynasty, moon, deity, or setting term.

Use Copy on one result or Copy batch for the visible list. The star button saves a name and its hook to that generator's browser-local shelf; Saved switches between fresh results and the shelf. Favorites stay on the current computer and browser profile, not in the exported game.
